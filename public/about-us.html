Our Story

NJ RG Shopziee LLC, founded in 2021, emerged with a vision to revolutionize e-commerce by bringing premium products to customers worldwide. Based in Clifton, New Jersey, we've grown from a passionate team into a trusted digital marketplace serving customers globally.

Silvera represents our commitment to excellence—precious, trusted, and refined, just like the experience we deliver to every shopper. We recognized a gap in the retail landscape: customers wanted convenience, variety, security, and reliability all in one place. We built Silvera to fill that gap.

Today, we've expanded our offerings to include everything from fashion and electronics to home essentials and beauty products. Our success is built on three pillars: Customer-First Service, Quality Assurance, and Secure Payment Solutions. We partner with trusted local and international brands to ensure our customers receive authentic, high-quality products at competitive prices.

Our partnership with DirectPay enables us to offer 15+ payment options—from local Philippine e-wallets like GCash and PayMaya to international cards and digital wallets. We're committed to making shopping safe, easy, and accessible to customers worldwide. Proud to serve the Philippines with the same quality and dedication.
